Acuity Tester is a website designed to help automate the interview process. Interviewers are able to create simple quizzes which serve as a way to filter out applicants who are unlikely to make it to the later stages of the interview process. Acuity Tester works great for small teams or startups that may not have the time and/or resources necessary to interview every applicant, or are unable to obtain licensing for a similar proprietary product.

Acuity Tester is not just for those looking for a few helpings hands. Here, prospectful applicants can come to practice for interviews using some of our hand-crafted quizzes. Afterwards, they can see how they rank up against other applicants who have taken the same quiz and figure out which skills they need to improve on to be competitive in the market.
